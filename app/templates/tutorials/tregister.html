Registration

Registration is a required part of using TTracker3. By registering you can create/edit tickets and potentially access admin features. This tutorial will explain the registration process, which should be fairly easy.


  1. Click the "Register" link on the right side of the navigation bar.
  2. Fill out the information the form asks for. All fields are required

    3. Consider the Following

    • Your first and last name are displayed publicly and are more than likely used by your instructor.
    • Your email is used for logging in purposes only; TTracker3 will never email you.
    • Your password must be between 6 and 30 characters. There are no complexity requirements.
  3. Click the "Register" button to submit your information. If you are brought back to the homepage with a success message, you may now log in. If you recieved error messages, address the problems and try again.
