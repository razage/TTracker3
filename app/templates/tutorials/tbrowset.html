Browsing Tickets

Browsing tickets is obviously an important part of this system. It allows technicians to see what others did to fix various problems. It also allows the administrators to see what their technicians have been up to.


  1. Once you are logged in, click the "Tickets" option on the navigation bar. This will show an additional menu of options.
  2. Select the "Browse Tickets" option.
Additional Information

You may notice that each ticket is colored a certain way. These colors correspond to the current status of the ticket. Here is a key for what each color represents:

  • Unclaimed
  • Repairing
  • Waiting
  • Complete
